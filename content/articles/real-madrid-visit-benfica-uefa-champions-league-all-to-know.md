## Real Madrid Visit Benfica: UEFA Champions League - All to Know
The UEFA Champions League is heating up, and one of the most anticipated matches of the season is just around the corner. Real Madrid, the Spanish giants, are set to visit Benfica, the Portuguese powerhouse, in a crucial Group Stage encounter. * Real Madrid are currently top of Group F with 10 points from 4 matches.
* Benfica are second in the group with 7 points from 4 matches.

* Benfica have been in impressive form at home, winning 5 of their last 6 matches at the Estádio da Luz.

The UEFA Champions League is the pinnacle of club football, and this match promises to be an exciting encounter between two of Europe's top teams. Benfica, on the other hand, have been playing a 4-2-3-1 formation, with a focus on quick transitions and exploiting the opposition's weaknesses. Their squad is led by talented players like Gonçalo Ramos and João Mário.
