## Barcelona 3-0 Real Oviedo: Blaugrana Return to La Liga Summit
Barcelona have regained their position at the top of the La Liga table following a convincing 3-0 victory over Real Oviedo at the Camp Nou. The Catalan giants were in dominant form, with goals from Dani Olmo, Raphinha, and Lamine Yamal securing all three points.

### Key Highlights:
* Barcelona move back to the top of La Liga with 3-0 win over Real Oviedo
* Dani Olmo, Raphinha, and Lamine Yamal score for the Blaugrana
* Real Oviedo remain in the relegation zone after another defeat
* Barcelona's win puts pressure on title rivals to respond

### Introduction
Barcelona's return to the top of La Liga was marked with a resounding 3-0 win over Real Oviedo, as Xavi's side demonstrated their intent to reclaim the Spanish title. The Blaugrana were in scintillating form, with their attacking trio of Olmo, Raphinha, and Yamal causing havoc for the Oviedo defense. The win moves Barcelona back to the summit of La Liga, and they will be hoping to maintain their position in the coming weeks.

### Tactical Analysis / Context
Xavi's decision to start Olmo, Raphinha, and Yamal paid dividends, as the trio combined to devastating effect. Olmo's goal, which opened the scoring, was a testament to his vision and precision, while Raphinha's strike showcased his pace and power. Yamal's goal, which sealed the win, highlighted his clinical finishing and composure in front of goal. The Blaugrana's midfield trio of Pedri, De Jong, and Gavi controlled the tempo of the game, allowing their attacking counterparts to flourish.
